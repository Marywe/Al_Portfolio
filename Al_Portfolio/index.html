PROJECTS

TIME KILLER
TAGS: Unity, Designer, Programmer, Artist
Time Killer is a 3D platformer where you have to prove your skill by completing levels in a race against time.
In each level, you have a time limit to complete it. The time you have left over in each level will accumulate in your reserve, and you will be able to use it in future levels.
COOL THINGS I DID IN THIS PROJECT:
  • Creation of the game concept.
  • Design and programming of a quick and responsive 3D controller which works with any camera angle.
  • Fast, cool, and optimized level progression.
  • Online capabiilities with a registration system and a leaderboard (and the posibility of still playing offline).
  • Deal with great amount of optimizations for a target 60fps build on Web.
  • Concept, 3D modeling, texture paint, rigging and modeling.
MYRMICA
TAGS: Designer, Programmer, Artist
Myrmica is an investigation and puzzle adventure viewed from an isometric perspective. Explore a petrol station while chased by a time anomaly.
Manipulate the routines of 5 unique characters to achieve your goal, create your own scenarios while trapped in a time loop, and uncover the truth of Complex B13B.
COOL THINGS I DID IN THIS PROJECT:
  • Designing puzzles combined with the narrative. Progression and difficulty design.
  • Creation GDDs for the gameplay, and write and record tutorials of implementation tutorials for other designers.
  • Write technical documentation for programmers, being the bridge between Design and Programming teams.
  • Blueprint programming and bug fixing.
  • Assets implementation.
  • Creating presentations and showing them to the publisher.
  • Creation of shaders and materials, 3D modeling.
YO, CREATURETO
TAGS: Unity, Designer, Programmer
Yo, Creatureto is an arcade runner game based on the #holdmybeer section of the famous spanish podcast “Yo, Interneto”, in colaboration with Mahou 5 Estrellas.
In this game, you will play as the “Creaturas” which are some kind of Frankenstein-like creatures created by different pieces of iconic videogame characters.
COOL THINGS I DID IN THIS PROJECT:
  • Create a game for and negotiate with a larger client.
  • Create and integrate product placement.
  • Design and programming of a quick and arcade-like experience.
  • Make the game playable in web, mobile and desktop.
  • Registration system and a leaderboard.
  • Set-up and rigging of 2D characters.
TÍTULO
TAGS: Unity, Designer, Programmer, Artist
The best years of your life is a serious simulation game where you will have to take care of your character while facing the university stage.
